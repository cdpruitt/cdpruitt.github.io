Biography

I’m a PhD candidate in Nuclear Chemistry at Washington University in St Louis. My current academic work includes measurement of neutron cross sections on isotopically-enriched targets, advancing the Dispersive Optical Model (DOM) framework, and constraining the density dependence of the nuclear symmetry energy, neutron skin thicknesses, and the isovector dependence of optical potentials.

I co-directed/produced THE SURROUNDING GAME (2018), the first feature documentary about the game of Go (available on iTunes, Amazon, Google Play, Vimeo, etc., and released by Netflix internationally in Aug 2018).
